Once upon a time there was a frog

She was a very beautiful frog but very lonely

The frog decided to go to the ball in the swamp.

She was about to wear her beautiful dress but she didn't realize that she gained weight after lockdown.

The dress could barely close but she managed by using duct tape

At the ball she ate some delicious time flies - woah it's 3 AM, what happened?

9 PM Arrive at the ball, eat flies and drink champagne because it's easier than talking to people
10 PM  Get tipsy and more self-confident and talk to old frogs from school
11 PM  Swamp duck steals ducktape (!!!)
midnight  
1 AM  She hid in the bushes and made a dress out of big leafs
2 AM  When she came out again, every one was watching her, thinking: "I love that dress"
3 AM  Her confidence got a boost and she started dancing.
